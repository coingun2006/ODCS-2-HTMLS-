Four turtles named Leonardo, Donatello, Raphael, and Michelangelo are trained by Master Splinter to become ninja heroes.
They live in the sewers and fight villains like the Shredder to keep New York City safe.

Ben Tennyson finds a strange alien watch called the Omnitrix that lets him transform into different alien heroes.
With his cousin Gwen and Grandpa Max, Ben saves the world from alien threats and villains.

Brothers Chris and Martin Kratt use their creature power suits to learn about animals and protect them from people who harm the environment.
Every episode mixes fun adventure with real science facts!

Aang, the last Airbender and Avatar, must master the four elements — Water, Earth, Fire, and Air — to bring peace to the world and defeat the Fire Nation’s tyranny.

Finn the Human and Jake the Dog go on wild adventures across the magical Land of Ooo.
They meet strange characters and face challenges filled with humor, friendship, and imagination.
