Ben 10 The Ben 10 synopsis follows Ben Tennyson, a 10-year-old boy who discovers the Omnitrix, a powerful alien device that allows him to transform into various alien species. While traveling with his cousin Gwen and Grandpa Max during summer vacation, Ben uses the Omnitrix to fight evil forces from Earth and space, growing from an immature child into a heroic leader by learning to wield the responsibility of his powers.
Total Drama Island Total Drama Island is a reality competition parody series where 22 teenagers compete in dangerous and disgusting challenges on a rundown island in Muskoka, Ontario, to win $100,000.
Avengers Earth Mightest Heroes Earth's Mightiest Heroes is an animated television series where Earth's most powerful superheroes, including Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk, and Ant-Man, form a team to combat major threats like Loki, Kang the Conqueror, and Ultron, and to defend the planet from various supervillain breakouts and alien invasions
Iron man Aurmoured Adventures Iron Man: Armored Adventures follows teenage engineering prodigy Tony Stark, who becomes the superhero Iron Man after a plane crash leaves him with a damaged heart and his father dead. With his best friends, James "Rhodey" Rhodes and Pepper Potts, Tony uses a high-tech suit of armor he invented to fight crime
Jackie chan Adventures Jackie Chan Adventures is an animated series following a fictionalized, archaeologist version of Jackie Chan as he protects magical talismans from the criminal organization The Dark Hand, and other supernatural threats, alongside his adventurous niece Jade, and wise-cracking Uncle, a skilled antiquarian.
Spectalcular Spider-Man The Spectacular Spider-Man is an animated series focusing on the early years of a teenage Peter Parker balancing his high school life and responsibilities as Spider-Man after a genetically altered spider bite gives him powers. He navigates friendships, school, and a budding relationship with Gwen Stacy, all while protecting New York City from villains like the Vulture and facing the challenges of keeping his secret identity.
