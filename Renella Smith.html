My Favourite Cartoons From My Childhood

Cartoon Name Synopsis Theme song + Picture
South Park In South Park, Colorado, Stan, Kyle, Kenny, and Eric are four friends with mischievous adventures on the agenda. The boys face supernatural and uncanny events that bring out the gullible stupidity of the adults around them. Between terrorizing each other and shocking members of their town, the scandalous schoolboys are often the unlikeliest source of truth and reason.
Family Guy Peter Griffin is your average American family guy. He has a wonderful wife, Lois, three children – Chris, Meg and Stewie – and a pseudo-intellectual talking dog, Brian. Expect plenty of goofball adventures and pop culture satire as the Griffin family live out their daily lives in the fictional city of Quahog, Rhode Island.Family Guy is an animated sitcom created by Seth MacFarlane, who is also the creator of American Dad! and Ted.
Total Drama Total Drama Island focuses on twenty-two teenagers' arrival at Camp Wawanakwa to compete on a reality television show. The contestants are divided into two teams and must compete in challenges every three days. While the winning team earns invincibility, the losing team has to vote off one of their own players. Whoever is voted off must walk the Dock of Shame to the Boat of Losers and leave the island. The teams eventually dissolve and the elimination process continues until the last contestant standing wins a grand prize of $100,000.
Scooby Doo Scooby Doo follows the adventures of a group of teenagers, Fred, Daphne, Velma, Shaggy, and their talking dog , Scooby-Doo, who travel around in their van, the Mystery Machine, solving mysteries. Each story typically begins with the gang arriving in a new town or location where strange, seemingly supernatural events are happening which consists of ghosts, monsters, or other spooky creatures scaring people away. The gang investigates, using clues, logic, and teamwork (often with plenty of comic mishaps from Scooby and Shaggy).
Tom and Jerry Tom and Jerry is a series that centers on a friendship/rivalry (a love-hate relationship) between the title characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse, where the house cat named "Tom" and the mouse named "Jerry" try to outsmart eachother in a cycle of cat and mouse chase.
