Pink Panther The Pink Panther is a funny animated show about a silent pink cat who gets into all sorts of situations. Using clever tricks and expressions instead of words, he always finds smart ways to solve problems or outsmart others.
Lazy town 8-year-old Stephanie moves to LazyTown to live with her uncle, she encourages her new friends to go outside and play, instead of sitting inside playing video games. Her efforts are aided when her uncle, Mayor Meanswell, asks for the help of superhero Sportacus, who urges citizens of LazyTown to stay fit and healthy and eat "sports candy"(otherwise known as fruits and vegetables). However, Villain Robbie Rotten works to stop their efforts, preferring LazyTown citizens to be quiet and lazy by watching the town from his underground lair.
The Amazing World of Gumball A 12-year-old cat, Gumball Watterson, stays in Elmore and has a penchant for getting into trouble along with his best friend, a fish named Darwin.
Tom and Jerry The series features comic fights between an iconic set of adversaries, a house cat (Tom) and a house mouse (Jerry). The plots of many shorts are often set in the backdrop of a house, centering on Tom (who is often enlisted by a human) trying to capture Jerry, and the mayhem and destruction that follows.
Totally Spies! Under the direction of the World Organization of Human Protection's director Jerry, three Beverly Hills teens are undercover agents. Alex, Sam and Clover fight international crime and save the world. Because they're secret spies the girls still have to attend school and keep their grades up while battling evil.
