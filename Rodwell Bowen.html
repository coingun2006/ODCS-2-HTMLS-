Fairly odd parents Timmy Turner is the main protagonist of the Nickelodeon cartoon The Fairly OddParents. He is a 10-year-old boy who lives in Dimmsdale, with his idiotic parents, who refuse to believe that his babysitter Vicky is evil until Channel Chasers, and he has fairy godparents named Wanda and Cosm
Invader zim Invader Zim is an American animated science fiction dark comedy television series created by Jhonen Vasquez for Nickelodeon. The series follows Zim, an extraterrestrial from the Irken Empire, who is determined to conquer Earth and enslave its inhabitants.
Jimmy Neutron Jimmy Neutron is a young boy genius who invents gadgets to solve problems. He often finds himself in adventures with his friends Carl and Sheen.
Planet Sheen Planet Sheen is a spin-off of The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius, focusing on the character Sheen Estevez as he navigates life on the alien planet of Zeenu.
The Simpsons The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company. The series is a satirical depiction of working-class life epitomized by the Simpson family, which consists of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie.
