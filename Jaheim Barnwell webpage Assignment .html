My Favorite Cartoons

Lion king Princess and The Frog Ice Age Aladin Tarzan
The story takes place in the Pride Lands of Africa and follows a young lion named Simba, who is destined to become king after his father, Mufasa. However, Simba’s evil uncle Scar plots to take the throne by tricking Simba into thinking he caused his father’s death. Heartbroken, Simba runs away and grows up far from home with two funny new friends, Timon and Pumbaa.
Tiana a hardworking young woman who dreams of opening her own restaurant. Her life changes when she meets Prince Naveen, who has been turned into a frog by an evil voodoo magician named Dr. Facilier.
Ice Age, when huge glaciers covered much of the Earth. It follows a group of prehistoric animals who form an unlikely friendship while trying to return a lost human baby to its family.
Aladdin, a poor but kind-hearted young man who lives in the fictional city of Agrabah. He finds a magic lamp that releases a powerful and funny Genie, who grants him three wishes.
John Clayton, a boy orphaned by a shipwreck in the African jungle and raised by gorillas, who grows into a skilled wild man known as Tarzan.
