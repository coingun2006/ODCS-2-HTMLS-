My Favorite Cartoon Characters

Character Image Description
Mickey Mouse Mickey Mouse is a cheerful and adventurous character created by Walt Disney. He is curious, optimistic, and always ready for fun. Mickey often goes on exciting adventures with his friends Minnie, Donald, and Goofy. He has become one of the most iconic cartoon characters worldwide and is considered a symbol of joy and imagination.
SpongeBob SquarePants SpongeBob is a funny, optimistic sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea in Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and loves making Krabby Patties. SpongeBob is loyal, energetic, and often gets into silly adventures with his best friend Patrick. Despite his clumsy nature, he always tries to see the bright side of life and never gives up on his dreams.
Bugs Bunny Bugs Bunny is a clever, witty rabbit famous for his catchphrase "What’s up, Doc?". He is known for outsmarting his rivals, especially Elmer Fudd, with his intelligence and humor. Bugs often stays calm even in tricky situations and uses his sharp wit to turn the tables. With his mischievous charm and love of carrots, he has become one of the most beloved cartoon icons of all time.
Tom Tom is a house cat from the classic series *Tom and Jerry*. He is mischievous, determined, and constantly tries to catch Jerry the mouse. Although he rarely succeeds, Tom’s wild chases lead to hilarious and entertaining situations. Despite being rivals, Tom sometimes shows kindness toward Jerry, making their relationship both funny and surprisingly heartwarming.
Jerry Jerry is a small but clever mouse who lives in the same house as Tom. He is resourceful, playful, and always finds creative ways to escape Tom’s traps. Jerry’s intelligence and bravery make him a lovable character, and he often teaches Tom lessons through his clever tricks. Although he enjoys teasing Tom, Jerry sometimes helps him in moments of trouble, showing that their rivalry is also a friendship at heart.
