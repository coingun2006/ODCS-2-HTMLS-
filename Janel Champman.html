Teen Titans Five teen superheroes unite to protect their city from villains while navigating the challenges of youth. A team of five young superheroes—Robin, Starfire, Cyborg, Raven, and Beast Boy—combine their unique powers to fight crime and protect Jump City while facing the challenges of growing up and building friendship. Watch on YouTube
Tom and Jerry Tom and Jerry is an American animated franchise and series of comedy short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. Best known for its 161 theatrical short films by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, the series centers on a friendship/rivalry (a love-hate relationship) between the title characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse. Many shorts also feature several recurring characters. Watch on YouTube
Family Guy Peter Griffin is your average American family guy. He has a wonderful wife, Lois, three children – Chris, Meg and Stewie – and a pseudo-intellectual talking dog, Brian. Expect plenty of goofball adventures and pop culture satire as the Griffin family live out their daily lives in the fictional city of Quahog, Rhode Island.Family Guy is an animated sitcom created by Seth MacFarlane, who is also the creator of American Dad! and Ted Watch on YouTube
Lilo and Stitch An intergalactic alien fugitive crash lands on Earth and meets Lilo, a young Hawaiian girl. The pair strike up a powerful bond of friendship, and Lilo introduces her new companion to Hawaiian culture, softening his single-mindedness. Meanwhile, authorities from across the galaxy are searching for the missing genetic experiment that Lilo has named Stitch. Watch on YouTube
Barbie life in the dream house The series is set as a reality TV-esque show following Barbie, her sisters and her friends in the day-to-day activities that take place in the Dreamhouse and surrounding areas. Much of the humor in the show derives from parodying and lampooning both the traditional reality TV format and the Barbie franchise itself. Watch on YouTube
